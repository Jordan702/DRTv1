Decentralized Resource Token – Evolution Through Contribution

DRTv1 is a revolutionary digital currency designed to reward the creation of real-world value. Built on blockchain and guided by the principle of "Evolution Through Contribution," DRTv1 aims to empower individuals and communities by compensating meaningful actions—healing, feeding, teaching, building, and restoring.

This open-source, ERC-20 token exists to foster a more just and sustainable economy—one where contributions are transparent, verifiable, and rewarded without reliance on debt or scarcity.
